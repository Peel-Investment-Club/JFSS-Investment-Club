JFSS Investment Club

Featuring informative lessons, competitions, and guest speakers, the Investment Club at John Fraser aims to spread a shared passion for business and investing; oroudly a registered chapter of the Peel Investment Club.

About Us

With over 100 members, the investment club at John Fraser Secondary School aspires to get students excited about business and investing. This is accomplished in many ways, primarily through informative lessons, talks held by guest speakers and alumni from the top business schools in Canada, and engaging competitions such as our year-long stock market simulator.

We strive on diversity and inclusiveness, whether you’re an expert investor or are a beginner you’re welcome to join the club! If you’re just getting started, we have many resources available to help you in your journey.
